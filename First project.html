ROYAL ENFIELD

Café Racer

The updated Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 takes
its inspiration from the 1970s era of British motorcycling,
with its cast alloy wheels and blacked-out aesthetic.
Alongside its classically-styled, ‘50s and ‘60s-inspired predecessors,
it recaptures the spirit of the cafe racer culture of the time
and that of our own Continental GT 250.
With its sculpted tank, eager attitude and
tucked-in riding position, it carries all the hallmarks of a traditional
café racer making it perfect for carving up
the back roads or having a ton of fun in the city.
